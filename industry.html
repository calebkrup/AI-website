1. Business Description

oVRcome operates within the healthcare industry, specializing in the integration of virtual reality (VR) technology into mental health treatment. The company provides VR-based exposure therapy and relaxation training to help clients overcome anxiety, phobias, and other mental health challenges through immersive therapeutic environments.

2. My Role in the Company

As a counselor/therapist at oVRcome, I work directly with clients to assess their needs, guide them through VR therapy sessions, and track their progress over time. I collaborate with a multidisciplinary team to design customized treatment plans and ensure that each client receives safe, evidence-based, and effective therapeutic experiences.

3. Ethical Issue

One ethical issue that could arise in my role is the protection of client confidentiality during virtual sessions. Because VR therapy involves digital platforms and sensitive personal data, there is a risk of unauthorized access or accidental sharing of confidential health information. Maintaining privacy and informed consent is crucial to uphold ethical standards in counseling.

4. Cybersecurity Issues

  • Data Breaches: Client mental health records and VR session data could be exposed if the system is hacked or improperly secured.
  • Phishing and Social Engineering: Cybercriminals could impersonate staff or IT support to gain access to sensitive systems or client data.

5. Cybersecurity Best Practices

  • Use multi-factor authentication and strong passwords for all logins.
  • Encrypt client data both in transit and at rest.
  • Provide staff training on recognizing phishing and social engineering attempts.
  • Regularly update VR software and cybersecurity tools to patch vulnerabilities.
  • Follow HIPAA and other data protection regulations to maintain client confidentiality and system integrity.
